  • 1
    Adding a Custom Domain
    Go to Settings ‚Üí Domains ‚Üí Add Domain. Enter the domain (e.g., yoursite.com) and follow the DNS instructions. It can take 24‚Äì48 hours to fully activate.
  • 2
    Setting Up Email (SMTP or LC Email)
    GHL has built-in LC Email or you can use a custom SMTP (like SendGrid, Mailgun). Go to Settings ‚Üí Email Services. Using LC Email is the easiest option to start.
  • 3
    Email Verification (DNS Records)
    Add SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records to your domain's DNS. These records prove to email providers that your emails are legitimate, drastically improving deliverability.
  • 4
    Test Your Setup
    After setup, send a test email to yourself. Check that it lands in the inbox (not spam), displays your correct "from" name, and all links work properly.
