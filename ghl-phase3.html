Workflow Triggers

A trigger is the event that starts the workflow. Choose the right trigger for what you want to automate.

üìã
Form & Survey Triggers
  • Form Submitted
  • Survey Submitted
  • Great for lead capture forms
  • Triggers instantly when form is filled
üìÖ
Appointment Triggers
  • Appointment Booked
  • Appointment Status Changed
  • Appointment Reminder (time-based)
  • No-show handling
üè∑Ô∏è
Contact Triggers
  • Tag Added to Contact
  • Tag Removed from Contact
  • Contact Created
  • Pipeline Stage Changed
üí¨
Communication Triggers
  • Customer Replied (SMS or Email)
  • Email Opened or Clicked
  • Missed Call
  • Inbound Call Started
üí°
Important: You can add multiple triggers to one workflow. For example, trigger a welcome sequence when either a form is submitted OR a tag is added ‚Äî whichever happens first.
